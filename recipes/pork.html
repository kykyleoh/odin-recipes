Pork Belly Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Star Anise
  • Leeks
  • Whole black pepper
  • Chicken cubes

Steps

  1. Boil pork in water with star anise, garlic, leeks, and whole peppers.
  2. After 2 hours, remove pork and immediately poke skin with fork.
  3. Pre heat an oven to 150C and put pork in with skin side up
  4. Cook for 2 hours or until golden brown, then save last 30 minutes and increase temperature to 200C to crisp up the skin
