Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Dry Spaghetti No.5
  • Egg Yolks
  • Grana Padano
  • Guanciale

Steps

  1. Fry your guanciale in a pan without oil until it is slightly golden brown. Set aside after.
  2. Mix 5 egg yolks and 1 egg white along with your grated grana padano cheese
  3. Boil pasta in salty water until al dente around 7 minutes
  4. Drain pasta then add guanciale and egg yolk and cheese mixture, then add pasta water. Cook until sauce reduces and coats pasta.
